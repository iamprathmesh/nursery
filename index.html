OUR SERVICES

Green brooks believes that planting a garden is to believe in tomorrow!

Pruning

After 2-3 months we provide the facility of pruning , wherein we trim the unwanted branches which improves the tree's structure and direct new, healthy growth.

Pesticides and fertilizers

Depending on the condition of plants , we spray pesticides once in two months to protect plants against pests. Fertilizers high in nitrogen will be provided to the costumers for a minimal cost from the day of purchase of the sapling for its healthy growth.
