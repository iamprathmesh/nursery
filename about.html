ABOUT US

We are a Delhi based plant nursery and currently have branches all over India.

In 2020, we were honoured as the 2nd most leading plants service provider in India. Our moto is to make this planet green by providing as many plants as we can to the consumers. Hence we have launched our campaign of " Bringing nature to your Doorstep " wherein we deliver plants online and also provide complimentary servives to our dear consumers.

Quality Products

Most of the people hesitate to buy or order plants online because it is not possible to check the quality of the plants physically. The talk is real and genuine, but we want to throw some light on this. Online plant sellers are also doing business, and if they don't provide quality products, the business won't survive for long.

Choice In Vases

We believe that this only benefit, boosts up the charm of ordering plants online by thousand times. Plants not just provide health benefits but also uplift the home decor. The variety of plant vases that you can enjoy online is something that local nurseries will never be able to match. A plant potted in a beautiful vase can make your home look great.

Less Expensive

Just like shopping from many e-commerce portals, ordering plant online is also less expensive. The competition is really high between online nurseries which leads to competitive prices of all types of plants. Also, Green Brooks offer a discount through coupons and promotional codes from time to time.

Variety

Don't we all love to have lots and lots of options while purchasing anything? The benefit of ordering plants online is that you will have plenty of options to choose from. From flowering plants to medicinal plants, options are countless. The choice of plants available in the physical/local plant nursery is limited and outdated, owing to several factors. Isn't that a countable benefit?

OUR SERVICES

Green brooks believes that planting a garden is to believe in tomorrow!

Pruning

After 2-3 months we provide the facility of pruning , wherein we trim the unwanted branches which improves the tree's structure and direct new, healthy growth.

Pesticides and fertilizers

Depending on the condition of plants , we spray pesticides once in two months to protect plants against pests. Fertilizers high in nitrogen will be provided to the costumers for a minimal cost from the day of purchase of the sapling for its healthy growth.

Vertical gardens

If you are struggling with the problem of not having sufficient place for your plants , we have now started our own vertical gardens! These green wall can transform empty space into aesthetically pleasing and creatively stimulating eye candy. So what are you waiting for!

Artificial Lawns

If you are thinking of creating an artificial garden in your terrace, Green Brooks is the right one for that! We have different kinds of artificial lawns which are life lasting and convenient to have
